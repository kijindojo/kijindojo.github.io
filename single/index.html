Genbukan Kijin Dojo UK
Welcome to the Kijin Dojo website.

Kijin Dojo is dedicated to following the martial art traditions and techniques prescribed by Grandmaster Shoto Tanemura and is an official Dojo of the Genbukan World Ninpo Bugei Federation.

The martial art systems taught aim to give people the ability to use techniques for true case self-defence, not only of the body, but also for defense of the mind and spirit.

Classes are held in Bookham and Reading where new members are always welcome, see the training section for more details.

Training

What to expect

Regular Ninpo Taijutsu classes include rolling, breakfalls, punching, kicking, throwing and escape techniques. Special classes or seminars will also cover Chinese martial arts, sword drawing techniques, staff techniques and authentic Ryu Ha styles

What to bring:

When you arrive at your first class please wear loose clothing (or a Gi) and socks. If it is hot you may wish to bring a bottle of water and a small hand towel.

Grandmaster Shoto Tanemura

The Grandmaster of our organisation started training when he was just 9 years old in both unarmed and armed techniques. He has continued to train in many different schools since then and has made it his life's mission to preserve and teach true Japanese martial arts to the world.

For more details, please refer to the Genbukan main website

Instructors

Head Instructor

Laura Nakano holds 4th Dan in Ninpo Taijutsu , 4th Dan in Jujitsu, Shoden Menkyo in Mugen Shintou Ryu amongst other grades. She has been training in the Genbukan for over 25 years, 6 of which were spent living in Japan and training directly under the Grand Master.

Currently Laura teaches in Bookham, where she holds a regular kids class and monthly seminars

Instructor

Ross Whitby is 2nd Dan Ninpo Taijutsu and has been training under Laura since 2006 Ross started training in Kung Fu at the age of 12 and began teaching others at the age of 16. He initially studied Kickboxing, Taekwondo and wrestling before learning both Genbukan Ninpo Taijutsu to Instructor level

Ross teaches the weekly classes in Reading
