Classes

What to expect

All classes are lead by the teacher, and all students will be given instructions as to what in particular to practice. The general format of a class is as follows:

  • Warm up
  • Ceremonial bow in
  • Basic rolls and body evasions techniques
  • Basic kicks and punches
  • Students train with a partner in techniques from their Kyu level
  • Ceremonial bow out

What to bring:

When you arrive at your first class please wear loose clothing (or a Gi) and socks. If it is hot you may wish to bring a bottle of water and a small hand towel.
